How We Can Help You

Therapy techniques can help you manage your symptoms and gain effective coping strategies. Catalyst has individual providers who offer a variety of services to fit almost any need. These services include:

Trauma and PTSD Treatment

We offer Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing, or EMDR for trauma and PTSD. EMDR helps clients dealing with traumatic memories and PTSD.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, or CBT, helps to treat problems by identifying harmful emotions, behaviors, and thoughts and creating strategies to overcome them.

NeuroPsychology Evaluations

Neuropsychological testing is an important part of the treatment of traumatic brain injury, dementia, neurological conditions, and psychiatric disorders. This also includes concussion testing.

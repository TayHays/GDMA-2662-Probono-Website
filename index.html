Our Services

How We Can Help You

Catalyst has individual providers who offer unique services to fit almost any need. These services include:

EMDR for Trauma & PTSD

We offer EMDR for trauma and PTSD. Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing is a structured therapy which helps clients reduce the vividness and emotions associated with traumatic memories.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy is a form of psychotherapy that treats problems by modifying dysfunctional emotions, behaviors, and thoughts. CBT aims to identify harmful thoughts, assess whether they reflect reality, and employs strategies to overcome them.

NeuroPsych & Concussion Testing

Neuropsychological testing is an important component of the assessment and treatment of traumatic brain injury, dementia, neurological conditions, and psychiatric disorders. We also offer concussion testing.

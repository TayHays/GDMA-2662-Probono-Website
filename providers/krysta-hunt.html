About Krysta

Krysta has been practicing in Lincoln since 2014, and has been working in the health field since 2010. She believes every person has the potential to create positive change in their life, and works alongside clients to clarify their goals, identify their strengths, and help them decide on a plan to make lasting changes.

In her free time, Krysta keeps active by going on walks with her dog and doing Yoga. She enjoys church activities, family dinners, traveling, reading, watching movies, and playing board and video games.

About Eric

Eric is a licensed independent mental health practitioner as well as a provisionally licensed alcohol and drug counselor, practicing for two years in Lincoln. He aims to address mental health and substance abuse concerns from a trauma-informed approach. Eric is also a registered service provider for the state of Nebraska and has worked closely with the office of probation to provide services to those on probation with legal concerns.

Outside the office, Eric enjoys sports, camping, cooking, and spending time with his family.

About Christina

Christina has been practicing in Lincoln since 2014. She takes an open approach to therapy, creating a safe space for clients to share their experiences. She specializes in working with trauma survivors and those dealing with thoughts of suicide or self-harm. In an effort to provide counseling for clients that are unable to get to the office due to transportation difficulties, lack of childcare, being homebound, or living in a rural community, Christina provides online therapy services for those whose insurance covers telehealth sessions.

Christina loves being outdoors: camping, hiking, stargazing, and going on family walks with her husband and daughter. She enjoys spending time with friends, reading, playing board games, and traveling. She especially likes taking her deaf pup Washburn and blind pup Mickey to splash through the creek at the dog park.

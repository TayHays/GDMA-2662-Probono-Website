About Tracy

Dr. Kalnins is a Neuropsychologist practicing in Lincoln for nearly 25 years. She finds counseling clients to be rewarding, helping them find solutions to improve the quality of their life. She uses positive psychology to help her clients view situations as an opportunity for growth.

For the past five years, Dr. Kalnins has served on the Mental Health Board for Lancaster County. She loves serving her community and regularly gives professional presentations to a variety of audiences. She also serves on a Brain Injury Concussion Coalition to create better outcomes for students in Nebraska with mild traumatic brain injuries.

Dr. Kalnins spends her free time with family and friends, attending events for her three busy kids, and watching UNL football and volleyball. She likes to keep physically active, attends church to keep her spiritual life healthy, and believes in living life to the fullest.

About Kendra

Kendra is a Licensed Mental Health Therapist practicing in Lincoln since 1997. She has experience working with a variety of issues including ADHD, ODD, anxiety, depression, eating disorders, grief/loss, and adjustment issues.

Outside the office, Kendra enjoys trap shooting, traveling, Nebraska football, spending time with friends, and eating out.
